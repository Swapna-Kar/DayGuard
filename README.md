### Winter Daylight & Visibility Safety Advisor ###

DayGuard is a lightweight web application that helps users understand winter daylight conditions and visibility risks. It converts raw weather data into meaningful health and safety advisories for better daily planning.

## ğŸš© Problem Statement

During winter, reduced daylight hours, low temperatures, and foggy conditions negatively affect health, travel safety, and productivity. Existing weather applications provide numerical data but fail to explain its real-world impact. Users need a simple solution that interprets winter conditions and offers clear guidance.

## ğŸ’¡ Solution

DayGuard analyzes real-time weather and astronomical data to calculate daylight duration and visibility risk. It presents easy-to-understand health and safety advisories, helping users make informed decisions during winter.
